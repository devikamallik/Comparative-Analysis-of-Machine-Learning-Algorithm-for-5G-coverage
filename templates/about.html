    ABOUT THE PROJECT

    This study conducts a comparative analysis of various machine learning algorithms to predict 5G network coverage, focusing on identifying dominant feature parameters that influence coverage. The results highlight prediction accuracy across different models, offering insights into the most effective algorithms for 5G coverage forecasting.

    The project aims to analyze and compare the performance of various machine learning algorithms in predicting and optimizing 5G network coverage. With the rapid deployment of 5G networks, ensuring optimal coverage and capacity is critical to meet user demands and achieve efficient resource utilization.
